Welcome to Fever RSS Reader

This is a modern Python/Django implementation of the Fever RSS reader, maintaining full backward compatibility with the Fever API v3.

You can use this with iOS/macOS apps like Reeder, ReadKit, and other Fever-compatible RSS readers.

Getting Started

  • API Endpoint: {{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}/api/
  • Admin Panel: Manage feeds and groups
  • Your Email: {{ request.user.email }}

Configure Your RSS Reader

In your Fever-compatible app (like Reeder):

  • Server: {{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}
  • Email: Your registered email address
  • Password: Your account password
